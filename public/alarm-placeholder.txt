ALARM SOUND FILE REQUIRED

To enable the alarm sound in Anti-Doze detection:

1. Download or create an alarm sound file (WAV or MP3 format)
2. Name it either:
   - alarm.wav
   - alarm.mp3
3. Place it in the /public directory

You can download free alarm sounds from:
- https://freesound.org (search for "alarm" or "alert")
- https://www.zapsplat.com
- Or create your own

The component will automatically use the alarm sound when drowsiness is detected.
